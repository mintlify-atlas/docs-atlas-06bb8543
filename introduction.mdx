## What is Flowbaker?

Flowbaker Executor is a powerful workflow automation engine that runs on your machine and connects to the Flowbaker platform to execute workflows with various integrations. It enables you to automate complex tasks using integrations like Discord, GitHub, Gmail, Slack, OpenAI, and many more.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Executor">
    Download the binary or build from source, then run the setup wizard to configure your executor.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect to Workspace">
    Register your executor with the Flowbaker platform using a secure passcode-based workflow.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Workflows">
    Design workflows in the Flowbaker platform using a visual editor with drag-and-drop nodes.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Execute Automatically">
    Your executor receives and runs workflows assigned to it, handling all integrations locally.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Architecture

Flowbaker uses a distributed architecture where:

1. **Flowbaker Platform**: Cloud-based workflow designer and orchestrator
2. **Flowbaker Executor**: Local agent that runs on your infrastructure (this project)
3. **Integrations**: Node implementations that connect to external services

The executor polls for assigned workflows and executes them locally, ensuring your credentials and data remain on your infrastructure.

<Note>
  The executor runs an HTTP server on port `8081` for receiving workflow executions and health checks.
</Note>
