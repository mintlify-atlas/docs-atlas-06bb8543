## Overview

The Gmail integration allows you to interact with Gmail to send emails, manage drafts, organize messages with labels, and automate email workflows.

## Configuration

To set up the Gmail integration, you need to authenticate with OAuth:

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **OAuth Provider** | OAuth account for Gmail | Yes |

The integration uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with your Google account and supports custom OAuth configurations.

## Available Actions

### Send Mail

Send an email using your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **To**: Email address(es) of the recipient(s)
- **Cc**: Email address(es) to CC
- **Bcc**: Email address(es) to BCC
- **Subject**: The subject of the email
- **Body**: The body of the email

**Example:**
```json
{
  "to": ["recipient@example.com"],
  "subject": "Meeting Reminder",
  "body": "Don't forget our meeting tomorrow at 10 AM."
}
```

### Reply Mail

Reply to an email using your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email to reply to
- **Body**: The body of the reply email

### Find Mail

Find emails in your Gmail account using search queries.

**Parameters:**
- **Query**: The search query (use Gmail search syntax)
- **Limit**: Maximum number of results to return (1-500, default 10)

**Example Queries:**
- `from:sender@example.com`
- `subject:invoice`
- `is:unread after:2024/01/01`

### Get Mail

Get details of a specific email from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email to get

### Get Mails

Get multiple emails from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Limit**: Maximum number of results to return (1-500, default 10)

### Send Mail to Trash

Move an email to trash.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email to trash

### Delete Mail

Permanently delete an email from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email to delete

### Mark Mail as Read

Mark an email as read.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email to mark as read

### Mark Mail as Unread

Mark an email as unread.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email to mark as unread

### Create Draft

Create a draft email in your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **To**: Email address(es) of the recipient(s)
- **Cc**: Email address(es) to CC
- **Bcc**: Email address(es) to BCC
- **Subject**: The subject of the email
- **Body**: The body of the email

### Send Draft

Send a draft email from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Draft ID**: The draft ID of the email to send

### Get Draft

Get a draft email from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the draft to get

### Get Drafts

Get drafts from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Limit**: Maximum number of results to return

### Delete Draft

Delete a draft email from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Draft ID**: The draft ID of the email to delete

### Get Labels

Get labels from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Limit**: Maximum number of results to return

### Add Label

Add a label to your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Label Name**: The name of the label to add

### Remove Label

Remove a label from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Label ID**: The ID of the label to remove

### Add Label to Email

Add a label to an email in your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email
- **Label ID**: The ID of the label to add

### Remove Label from Email

Remove a label from an email in your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the email
- **Label ID**: The ID of the label to remove

### Get Thread

Get a thread from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Message ID**: The message ID of the thread

### Get Threads

Get threads from your Gmail account.

**Parameters:**
- **Limit**: Maximum number of results to return

## Available Triggers

### On Message Received

Triggered when a new message is received in your Gmail account.

## Example Workflows

### Auto-Reply to Emails

1. **Trigger**: On Message Received
2. **Action**: Reply Mail with automated response

### Email Organization

1. **Action**: Find Mail matching criteria
2. **Action**: Add Label to Email for organization
3. **Action**: Mark Mail as Read

### Draft Management

1. **Action**: Create Draft with email content
2. **Action**: Get Drafts to review
3. **Action**: Send Draft when ready

## Notes

- Some actions are only available with custom OAuth (not default OAuth)
- Message IDs can be obtained from trigger outputs or search results
- Gmail search syntax supports advanced queries for filtering
- Label IDs are different from label names
