## Getting Started

To use an integration in your workflow:

1. **Add Credentials**: Navigate to your workspace settings and add credentials for the integration you want to use
2. **Add Integration Node**: Drag the integration node into your workflow canvas
3. **Configure Actions**: Select the action you want to perform and configure its parameters
4. **Connect Nodes**: Connect the integration to other nodes in your workflow
5. **Test & Deploy**: Test your workflow and deploy it when ready
